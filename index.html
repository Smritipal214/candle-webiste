About Ruvya

At Ruvya, we believe every spark holds a story. "Ru" symbolizes soul and essence, while "Vya" reflects flow and emotion — together meaning a soulful light flowing into your space. Our candles are handcrafted moments of calm, warmth, and memory. Rooted in tradition, wrapped in modern minimalism.
